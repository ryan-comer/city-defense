PLAYABLE HEROES

Fire Hero
C class abilities
- Fire blasts
	- Auto attack fireballs (single target)
- Flamethrower
	- Move while create a cone of fire (AoE)

B class abilities
- Flight
- Flashbang
	- Stun and damage in an area around you

A class abilities
- Burning Blood
	- You heal base on damage you do for a period of time
- Blaze
	- Create a zone of fire that damages enemies caught within

S class abilities
- Heat Wave
	- Kamehameha like wave of fire
